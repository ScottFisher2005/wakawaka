# 📝 Weekly Lesson Plan

Welcome to the Magic Alphabet Sandbox! Here is our plan for the toddlers:

* **Monday:** Learning A, B, C and painting apples! 🍎
* **Tuesday:** Singing the alphabet song. 🎵
* **Wednesday:** Counting from 1 to 10. 🔢
* **Thursday:** Nap time and story reading. 📚
* **Friday:** Free play in the magical sandbox. 🧸

*Note for teachers: Ensure all magic wands (config files) are stored safely after class.*
